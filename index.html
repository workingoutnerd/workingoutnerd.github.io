Endurance Data Project: An Independent Research Initiative

Bio

Yongchul is a logistics professional with deep expertise in data-driven optimization and a personal passion for marathon running.

He is currently conducting independent research on the challenges and opportunities surrounding the use of performance data in endurance sports. This project aims to explore innovative solutions that can help empower athletes, coaches, and the broader running community.

Project Scope

The current focus of this research is to understand how running app developers, coaching platforms, and other technology companies access and utilize global race course data.

The project seeks to identify common pain points in data acquisition, quality, and implementation, with the goal of conceptualizing a more efficient and reliable solution for the industry.
